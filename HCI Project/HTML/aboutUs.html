ABOUT US

HISTORY

ERamen shop is one of the oldest ramen shops in the world. It was established since 1910 in Japan and brought to Indonesia since 2015. All Eramen menu made by organic flour and turn into delicious ramen noodle. Customer can find any kind of ramen, such as ramen and so many kind of tempura.

VISION & MISSION

ERamen is on a mission to spread the most authentic ramen around the world. With every bowl of ramen served, we will ensure that you get the best authentic classic ramen wherever you are. ERamen is a place that brings happiness to our customers, staff and people around us.
